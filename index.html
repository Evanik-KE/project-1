Welcome to the Sol Generation..

Present you the melodious vibes

Sauti Sol is a multi-award-winning Afro-pop band hailing from Nairobi, Kenya, that has taken the world by storm with their unique blend of soulful melodies, catchy rhythms, and powerful harmonies. Formed in 2005, the group quickly rose to prominence, becoming one of Africa's most celebrated musical acts. Consisting of four talented artists - Bien-Aime Baraza, Willis Austin Chimano, Savara Mudigi, and Polycarp Otieno - Sauti Sol has redefined the contemporary music scene and brought African sounds to the global stage.
