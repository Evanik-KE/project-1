Bien-Aime Baraza

Bien-Aime Baraza, the soulful
voice of Sauti Sol, captivates audiences with his emotive vocals. Co-founder of the iconic Kenyan band, he celebrates African identity through music, inspiring a global fanbase. His philanthropic efforts and musical evolution have solidified Sauti Sol's legacy as pioneers of modern Afro-pop. A true African musical gem.

Savara Mudigi

Savara Mudigi, a visionary member of Sauti Sol, brings rhythm and flair to the band's Afro-pop sound. As a skilled percussionist and vocalist, he amplifies their musical identity. Beyond the stage, Savara advocates for social causes, leaving a lasting impression on fans as an artist and a humanitarian.

Polycarp Otieno

Polycarp Otieno, a gifted guitarist and founding member of Sauti Sol, adds melodic brilliance to the band's eclectic tunes. With his exceptional musicality, he harmonizes traditional African elements with contemporary sounds. Polycarp's instrumental prowess and dedication have been instrumental in Sauti Sol's meteoric rise to global acclaim.
